# Hexagon Portal - Administrative User Manual

Welcome to the Hexagon Computer Systems management portal. This guide will help you navigate and manage your website content efficiently.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 🔓 Accessing the Dashboard
- **Login URL**: Navigate to `https://hexagonview.com/login` (or your local equivalent).
- **Authentication**: Enter your admin email and password.
- **Session**: For security, your session will expire after 24 hours of inactivity.

---

## 2. Navigating the Dashboard

The dashboard is organized into logical sections on the left sidebar:
- **Inquiries**: View and manage messages from potential clients.
- **Team**: Manage the staff members shown on the "Our Team" page.
- **Projects**: Portfolio management for your completed works.
- **Blog**: Post news, updates, and tech insights.
- **Services**: Update the core service descriptions and highlights.
- **Brands/Partners**: Manage logos of partners shown in the slider.
- **Clients**: Manage the list of organizations you serve.
- **Site Settings**: Core company info, social media, and security.

---

## 3. Managing Content

All management pages follow a **Unified Table Design**:
- **Search**: Use the top search bar to find records by name, title, or content.
- **Date Filter**: Select a date to see records created on that specific day.
- **Pagination**: Browse through records using the "Previous" and "Next" buttons at the bottom (15 items per page).

### 📝 Adding & Editing Records
1. Click **"+ New [Item]"** (e.g., + New Post) to add a record.
2. Fill in the required fields.
3. **Uploads**: Click the "Upload" button to select an image. It will be automatically optimized and stored in the cloud (Cloudinary).
4. Click **"Save"** to apply changes.

---

## 4. Blog & Project Best Practices

### 🖼️ Image Recommendations
- **Blog Covers**: Use landscape images (1200x630px recommended).
- **Project Images**: High-quality photos of your work (1000x1000px or 1200x800px).
- **Logos**: Use PNG format with transparent backgrounds for the best look.

### 🔍 SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
- Ensure every blog post has a descriptive **Excerpt**.
- Use relevant **Tags** (e.g., "Networking", "Addis Ababa", "IT Support").
- The **Slug** (URL) is automatically generated from the title, but you can customize it for better search rankings.

---

## 5. Site Settings & Security

### ⚙️ Company Information
Update your Phone, Email, Address, and TikTok links in the **Site Settings** panel. Changes here will update the **Header**, **Footer**, and **Contact Page** instantly.

### 🛡️ Security
To change your administrative password:
1. Go to **Site Settings**.
2. Scroll to the **Security** section.
3. Enter your current password and your new password twice.
4. Click **"Change Password"**.

---

## 6. Support & Troubleshooting

- **Image not appearing?** Check if your internet connection allows access to Cloudinary.
- **Form not sending?** Ensure the `.env` file on the server has the correct SMTP/Database credentials.
- **AI Agent not responding?** Verify that your Gemini API key is active.
